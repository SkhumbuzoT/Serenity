Our Story

Founded in 2015, Serenity Massage & Wellness was born from a passion for holistic healing and the transformative power of therapeutic touch. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, envisioned a space where clients could escape the stresses of daily life and reconnect with their bodies.

Today, our team of licensed therapists continues this mission, combining professional expertise with personalized care to create treatments that nourish both body and mind.

Our Philosophy

We believe in a client-centered approach where each session is tailored to your unique needs. Our techniques blend traditional massage therapy with modern wellness practices to deliver optimal results.

Certified by the National Certification Board for Therapeutic Massage & Bodywork
